What is WASD?

Wrist Analysis with Signal Decomposition

By analyzing the horizontal (y) wrist movements and frequencies while playing different genres of games (first-person-shooter, third-person-shooter, MOBA, etc), we hope to see which games result in the highest frequency wrist movement.

We are hoping to create a helpful resource that other gamers can reference to keep their wrist in good health.
